Hardware/Electrical Info
============================

About Our Swerve Modules
-------------------------

We are using the MK5 swerve modules from SJTU.

About Our Gyros
----------------

We use one gyro on our robot. The `Gadgeteer Pigeon <https://store.ctr-electronics.com/gadgeteer-pigeon-imu/>` IMU from Cross the Road Electronics (CTRE)
We have also test `NavX-MXP<>` on our robot and it works well.
